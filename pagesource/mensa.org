    <h2>What is Mensa?</h2>
 <p><a title="Members register here" href="/user/register"><img width="496" height="64" alt="Please take a look around the new Mensa International website" src="/sites/default/files/mensa_banner_i07_03.jpg" /></a></p>
<p>Mensa,&nbsp;the high IQ society,&nbsp;provides a forum for intellectual exchange among its members. There are members in more than 100 countries around the world.</p>
<p>Activities include the exchange of ideas through lectures, discussions, journals, special-interest groups, and local, regional, national and international gatherings; the investigations of members' opinions and attitudes; and assistance to researchers, inside and outside Mensa, in projects dealing with intelligence or Mensa.</p>
<p>This is how members see Mensa&nbsp;</p>
<p><img width="350" height="228" src="/sites/default/files/Mensa1Word_individuality.jpg" alt="Mensa one World image" />&nbsp;</p>
<div class="heading-replace">
<h3>How to join mensa</h3>
<img width="496" height="29" alt="How to join mensa" src="/sites/default/files/mensa_web_i24_03_38(1).gif" />
<p class="more-padding">Membership of Mensa is open to persons who have attained a score within the upper two percent of the general population on an approved intelligence test that has been properly administered and supervised.</p>
<hr />
<p><br />
<br />
We offer 2 ways to practise:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Our online <a title="Take the Mensa Workout quiz" href="workout">Mensa Workout</a> is an intelligence quiz in which you have half an hour to answer 30 questions. The Workout can't be used for qualification to join Mensa, but you can see how well you do!</li>
    <li>In some countries, a pre-test is available which you can take in the privacy of your home. To find out whether such a test is available in your country, please see <a href="national-groups">National Groups</a>.</li>
</ol>
