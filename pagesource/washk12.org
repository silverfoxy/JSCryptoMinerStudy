		 <a title="Use our Easy Sign Up to receive text messages via 'School Messenger'" href="/blog/item/please-opt-into-text-messaging-via-school-messenger">Use our Easy Sign Up to receive text messages via 'School Messenger'</a> 	</h3>
	
		<p class="meta">
		<span class="element element-itemauthor first last">
	Author Webmaster</span>	</p>
	
	
		<div class="description">
		<div class="element element-image first">
	
	<a href="/blog/item/please-opt-into-text-messaging-via-school-messenger"   title="Use our Easy Sign Up to receive text messages via &#039;School Messenger&#039;"  ><img src="http://www.washk12.org/cache/com_zoo/images/school_messenger_logo-1_9d12834352e2af77b956d7cadce5639c.jpg" title="Use our Easy Sign Up to receive text messages via &#039;School Messenger&#039;" alt="Use our Easy Sign Up to receive text messages via 'School Messenger'" width="450" height="100" /></a>
</div>
<div class="element element-textarea last">
	<div><p>To improve communication with parents, guardians and students, Washington County School District Technology expanded its ability to deliver phone, email and SMS text messages using SchoolMessenger. SchoolMessenger will be used to communicate in emergency situations as well as inform parents of day to day progress and school events.</p>
<p>We invite you to fully participate in the process by opting in to SMS Text messaging and keeping contact information in PowerSchool up to date. Just complete two simple steps:</p>
<ol>
<li>Opt in to SMS Messaging by sending the word Yes, via text message, to the number 67587. SchoolMessenger will only send text messages to cell phone numbers whose recipients have agreed to receive them.</li>
<li>Make sure you contact information is up to date by logging in to your PowerSchool Account and accessing the Demographics Change Page.</li>
</ol>
