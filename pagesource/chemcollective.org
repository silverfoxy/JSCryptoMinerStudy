	<p id="intro"> 
	   The ChemCollective is a collection of <a href="http://chemcollective.org/vlabs">virtual labs</a>, <a href="http://chemcollective.org/scenario_based"> scenario-based learning activities</a>, <a href="http://chemcollective.org/tutorials">tutorials</a>, and <a href="http://chemcollective.org/tests">concept tests</a>. Teachers can use our content for pre-labs, for alternatives to textbook homework, and for in-class activities for individuals or teams. Students can review and learn chemistry concepts using our virtual labs, simulations, and tutorials. The ChemCollective is organized by a group of faculty and staff at Carnegie Mellon
	   who are interested in using, assessing, and creating engaging online activities for chemistry education.
