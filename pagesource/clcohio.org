                        <section class="content-editor">
                            <h3><span style="line-height: 1.5;">The Central Library Consortium (CLC) was formed in 1988 to provide integrated library services and facilitate other cooperative projects that yield excellent quality and value to </span><a href="/member-libraries" target="_self" style="line-height: 1.5;">its members</a><span style="line-height: 1.5;">. The CLC continues to experiment with new models of service and resources sharing in response to the needs of its members and to keep pace with the ever changing technology in the library environment.</span></h3>
<h3>Goals</h3>
<ol>
<li>Use technology to provide the most appropriate information delivery system.</li>
<li>Use technology to control cost and improve the efficiency of library operations.</li>
<li>Maintain flexibility to adapt new and changing technology to meet the public's needs.</li>
<li>Evaluate emerging technology for effective responsiveness to the library's needs.</li>
<li>Use technology to connect to relevant sources outside the library.</li>
