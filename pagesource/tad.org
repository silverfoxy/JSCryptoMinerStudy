<h6><u><strong>PROPERTY SEARCH SCORING</strong></u></h6>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div><strong>Search Scoring</strong> utilizes the following criteria:</div>
<table style="margin-left: 40px;" width="300" cellspacing="1" cellpadding="1">
<tbody>
<tr class="no-border-row">
<td style="width: 150px;">Term Frequency</td>
</tr>
<tr class="no-border-row">
<td style="width: 150px;">Inverse Document Frequency</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p><strong>Term Frequency</strong> is the rate at which a term shows up in TADs database. The higher number of matches of a term, the higher up in the list it will display.</p>
<p><strong>Inverse Document Frequency</strong> is the rate at which the term you searched for shows up in documents. Unlike Term Frequency, this option shows up higher in the results list when there are less occurrences in the document. For example, if you searched for the word the you wouldnt get many results based on documents we have on file. If you searched for the word xylophone you would likely get a result from the documents based on that word.</p>
