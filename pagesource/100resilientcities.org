    <div class="row callout-content">
      <h4 class="callout-heading">Defining urban resilience</h4>
      <div class="callout-subheading">Resilience is about <strong>surviving and thriving</strong>, regardless of the challenge.</div>
    </div>
  </section>

  
  <section class="urban-resilience">
    <div class="small-12 large-6 columns urban-resilience_summary">
      <p><strong>Urban resilience</strong> is the capacity of individuals, communities, institutions, businesses, and systems within a city to survive, adapt, and grow no matter what kinds of chronic stresses and acute shocks they experience.</p>
<h4>CHRONIC STRESSES</h4>
<h6>weaken the fabric of a city on a day-to-day or cyclical basis</h6>
<p>Examples include: high unemployment, inefficient public transportation systems, endemic violence, and chronic food and water shortages.</p>
<h4>ACUTE SHOCKS</h4>
<h6>are sudden, sharp events that threaten a city</h6>
<p>Examples include: earthquakes, floods, disease outbreaks, and terrorist attacks.</p>

    </div>
