                            <div class="drop-down-content-wrapper">
                                <div class="dropdown-left-content">

                            <h2>Research, Development and Consultancy Division (RDCD)</h2>
                            <div class="drop-down-content-wrapper">
                               <p class="fifth-padding">The Research Development and Consultancy Division (RDCD) of the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations is entrusted with the responsibility of enhancing the educational and academic commitments of the Council.</p>
                                <br>
                                <p class="fifth-padding">The Research Development and Consultancy Division (RDCD) extends the operations of the Council beyond conduct of examinations, to wider frontiers of education and development, so as to help realise the Council's commitment towards qualitative improvement of education through optimizing various dimensions of pupil growth, the proficiency levels of teachers and enhancing the outcomes from institutional inputs.</p>
                                <br>
                                <p class="fifth-padding">Some of the activities undertaken by the Research Development and Consultancy Division of the Council include</p>
                                <br>
                                <p class="fifth-padding">
                                - Curriculum development and review including preparation of the ICSE, ISC and CVE syllabi.<br>
                                - Design and development of Specimen Question Papers.<br>
                                - Development of resource material for teachers and students.<br>
                                - Preparation of the documents entitled "Analysis of Pupil Performance" both at the ICSE and ISC levels.<br>
                                - Preparation of the Council's Quarterly magazine "The Council Record".<br>
                                - Conducting studies based on contemporary broad based academic issues.<br>
                                - Updating the Council's website.<br>
                                </p>

                    <h3 class="concessions-heading">Concessions to Children with<br />
                        Special Needs</h3>
                    <p>The Council is prepared to give special consideration and make necessary arrangements in respect of candidates who are handicapped in any way, which might affect their performance in the ICSE (X) / ISC (XII) Examination.</p>
                    <p>When a candidate suffers some injury or bereavement or dire unforeseen circumstance which may adversely affect his or her performance in the examination, the Special Difficulty procedure is used. A form is forwarded to the Council by the Head of the candidates school and the candidates examination answer script is then given special consideration by a committee before the issue of the results. The Councils committee does not give a "blanket" concession but treats every case on its merit, for experience has shown that candidates working under similar unusual stress are affected in widely different ways. For this reason the Special Difficulty Form provides for the performance of the affected candidate to be compared with that of his/her other classmates.</p>
