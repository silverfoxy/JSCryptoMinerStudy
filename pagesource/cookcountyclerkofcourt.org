		if (document.all(sWindow).style.display == 'none') {

			document.images(sWin).src = 'images/x.gif';
			document.images(sWin).alt = 'Collapse_This_Window';
			document.cookie = sWindow + "=TRUE";
		}
		else {
			document.all(sWindow).style.display = 'none';
			sWin = sWindow + "_option";
			document.images(sWin).src = 'images/maximize.gif';

	}

function txtDate_onKeypress()
{
	var string="1234567890/";
	if (string.indexOf(unescape('%' + window.event.keyCode.toString(16))) != -1) 
		window.event.returnValue = true;
	else
		window.event.returnValue = false;
}

The additional 20% in revenue was paid to state and local agencies.</p><p>In 2008, there were 1,705,413 new cases filed with the Office of the Clerk of the Circuit Court, and 19,773,061 case activity transactions were performed by Clerk&#146;s Office personnel. Our staff also handled 6,273,766 public inquiries.</p><p>As the Clerk of the Circuit Court of Cook County, my objective is making the Clerk&#146;s Office a customer service focused organization. I proudly bring you this web site to provide information and facilitate your interaction with the Clerk&#146;s Office and the court system. We will continue to improve this web site to ensure the highest level of customer service. Please check back periodically to see what&#146;s new.
