        <h3 style="text-align: center;">Proudly serving education in these Ohio counties&#8230;</h3>
<p><a href="http://www.noacsc.org/school-directory/"><br />
<img class="alignnone wp-image-3572 size-full" src="http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/noacsc_map-1.png" alt="" width="590" height="404" srcset="http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/noacsc_map-1.png 590w, http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/noacsc_map-1-300x205.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 590px) 100vw, 590px" /><br />
</a></p>
<p>The Northwest Ohio Area Computer Services Cooperative (NOACSC) is a regional computer services provider offering services to public and non-public school districts in northwest Ohio.</p>
<p>As one of 18 regional Information Technology Centers (ITC) that provide such services to the entire state of Ohio, we began in 1980 with the sole purpose of providing computer services to school district financial offices. Since then, services have been added that benefit school counselors, administrators, teachers, librarians, Fema students and parents.</p>
<p><center><a href="http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/ITC-Privacy-Policy-adopted-Feb-2016.pdf">Internet Privacy Policy</a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p><center><a href="http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/NOACSC-Data-Release-Policy.pdf">Data Release Policy</a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p><center><a href="http://www.noacsc.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/NOACSC-Open-Internet-Disclosure-Statement.pdf">Open Internet Disclosure Statement</a></center></p>
