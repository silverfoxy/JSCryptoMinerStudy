          <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/" class="active">Home</a> » Publications of the Japan Association for Language Teaching</div>          <div id="mission"><h2 class="text-center">
	JALT Publications</h2>
<h4>
	As a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting excellence in language learning, teaching, and research, <a href="http://www.jalt.org">The Japan Association for Language Teaching (JALT)</a>&nbsp; has a rich tradition of publishing relevant material in its many publications.</h4>
<p>This site is home to JALT&#39;s two main publications; <strong><em><a href="http://jalt-publications.org/tlt">The Language Teacher</a></em> (TLT)</strong> and <em><a href="http://jalt-publications.org/jj"><strong>JALT Journal</strong></a></em>, as well as the annual <a href="http://jalt-publications.org/proceedings"><em><strong>Postconference Publication</strong></em></a>. More information on JALT&#39;s publications can be found on the pages of this site.</p>
