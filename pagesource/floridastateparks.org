  <div class="views-field views-field-title">        <h3 class="field-content">Enjoy Floridas State Parks!</h3>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-body">        <div class="field-content"><p>Welcome to the website for Floridas state parks and state trails. This website will help you plan your visits to Floridas 175award-winning state parks, state trails and historic sites.</p>
<p>Two of our own Florida State Parks have made the Top 10 Beach List for 2017:Grayton BeachState Park in the Panhandle ranked #4 andCaladesi IslandState Park in Dunedin ranked #7. Florida State Parks include more than 100 miles of beaches and are a tremendous driver for the states tourism industry, creating jobs and economic opportunities.</p>
<p>The list is produced by coastal expert Dr. Stephen P. Leatherman, also known as Dr. Beach, a professor at Florida International University. Every year, since 1991, Dr. Beach has released the top 10 beaches using 50 criteria he created to rate the nations best beaches. Criteria to determine the ranking of the nations top beaches include beach material, water temperature, sand softness, smell, trash and litter, safety record and algae in the water.</p>
<p><strong><em>Stay in the know - follow us on <a href="http://www.facebook.com/flstateparks" target="_blank">Facebook</a>, <a href="http://www.twitter.com/flstateparks" target="_blank">Twitter</a>, <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/flstateparks" target="_blank">Pinterest</a>, <a href="http://www.instagram.com/fl.stateparks" target="_blank">Instagram</a>and <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCicot0DGo4GYo7nytmv2z9A" target="_blank">YouTube</a>. Also, be sure to download the PocketRanger app for iPhones and Android smart phones.</em></strong></p>
